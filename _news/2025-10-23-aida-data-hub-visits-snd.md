## {{ page.title }}
<span class="small">{{ page.date | date: "%Y-%m-%d"}}</span>

**AIDA Data Hub´s Data Science Director Joel Hedlund visited the [Swedish National Data Service (SND) network meeting in Malmö](https://snd.se/sv/natverkstraff-i-malmo-22-23-oktober-2025), to share experiences from using AI to facilitate access to metadata (that you didn't even know you had!).**

![Joel Hedlund presenting the AIDA Data Hub](/assets/images/snd_network_meeting.jpg){: .right width="40%"}

The assembled scientific data experts from SND got to enjoy an insight into semantic indexing and morphologic data mining with examples from [ASHA](/about/engagements/asha/) and [Bigpicture](https://bigpicture.eu), and visions for how this could contribute to [region common healthcare data management](/about/engagements/strategi-for-regionsgemensam-varddatahantering/).

The presentation is available here: [251023 Storskaligt användande av AI med fokus på tillgängliggörande av metadata för SND nätverksträff i Malmö](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1MWWQDHJi9maRCeB3xydGGSF4o-jwwSe2d0SdfuUQL04/edit?slide=id.g38c77035682_0_50#slide=id.g38c77035682_0_50)
