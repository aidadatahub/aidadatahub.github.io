## {{ page.title }}
<span class="small">{{ page.date | date: "%Y-%m-%d"}}</span>

**AIDA Data Hub and Region Östergötland (RÖ) has established agreements to use the AIDA Data Hub Data Science Platform (DSP) in the [ASHA project](https://www.vinnova.se/p/anvanda-standardiserade-halsodata-som-accelerator-for-att-starka-innovation-och-framtidens-halso--och-sjukvard/) on accelerating healthcare using standardized health data.**

The ASHA project aims to build a platform based on standardized health data to increase its use for innovation and development. By making data more accessible for clinical decision support, research, and product development, the initiative will help improve future healthcare and preventive efforts.
